Our History

HOW WE'VE STARTED OUR RESTAURANT

There was never a restraunt called "Lettuce Eat". It's just a frictional restraunt that serves the best Lettuce in the world. Mr. Sangeet Shrestha, The founder of this restaurant was given an assignment. The assignment was to create a website for his coursework by his teacher. And after few days of thinking, he decided to create a website for this restaurant and call it 'Lettuce Eat'.

Even though Lettuce Eat is an imaginary restaurant, it is real. The concept of creating a Vegeterian restauraunt named "Lettuce Eat" is authentic. The slogan "Be a cowboy, Not a cow eater" is also a very humorous way of telling the world to turn vegan.
